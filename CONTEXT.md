# Chinese Vocabulary Learning

This context describes the vocabulary learning experience for a university Chinese class. Learners practice each lesson's words by seeing, hearing, writing, and testing themselves.

## Language

**Chinese voice material**:
A fixed audio recording or generated audio asset for a vocabulary word's Chinese pronunciation. It is shared by pronunciation rather than owned by a lesson, and its reference is explicit so file names can stay readable and collision-resistant.
_Avoid_: Browser voice, device voice, speech synthesis voice

**Vocabulary word**:
A Chinese word assigned to a lesson, represented by hanzi, pinyin, Japanese meaning, and one required **Chinese voice material**.
_Avoid_: Item, card

## Example Dialogue

Teacher: "This vocabulary word needs a Chinese voice material before students use it in the listening test."

Developer: "If the Chinese voice material exists, the app plays that file for the vocabulary word instead of choosing a browser voice."
